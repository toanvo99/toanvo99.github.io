The home for college athlete influencers

Specially created for organizations & sponsors to follow and analyze individual athlete’s marketing performances

Download Download

Our team is creating a dashboard that will assist organizations by providing insights about athletes participating in their sponsored social media campaigns. The dashboard includes information such as audience location, reach and impressions for both athletes and campaigns. The resulting dashboard contributes to the start-up’s bigger effort to help collegiate athletes generate well-deserved income.

Opportunities

Student-athletes are looking for sponsorship, endorsement opportunities following NCAA rule changes regarding Name, Image and Likeness (NIL) rights

Resources

Inked Sports provides a bridge for the athletes and organizations/brands to connect and build relationships

Performance

Student-athletes are now able to acquire more recognition and additional sources of income by using their NIL

Campaign Dashboard Page

A Closer Look Into An Athlete’s Chart
